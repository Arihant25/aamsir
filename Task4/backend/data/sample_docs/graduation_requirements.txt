Graduation Requirements — Computer Science Department (CSD)

Acme University — Academic Year 2024-2025

1. Degree Requirements Overview
Students in the Computer Science Department must fulfill the following requirements to be eligible for graduation with a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Computer Science.

2. Credit Requirements
2.1 Minimum Total Credits: 128 credit hours
2.2 Core CS Credits: 64 credit hours from required CS courses
2.3 Mathematics Credits: 18 credit hours (Calculus I & II, Linear Algebra, Discrete Mathematics, Probability & Statistics)
2.4 Science Electives: 8 credit hours from approved science courses (Physics, Biology, Chemistry)
2.5 General Education: 24 credit hours across humanities, social sciences, and writing
2.6 Free Electives: 14 credit hours

3. Core CS Courses (Required)
- CS 101: Introduction to Computer Science (4 credits)
- CS 201: Data Structures and Algorithms (4 credits)
- CS 210: Computer Organization (4 credits)
- CS 301: Operating Systems (4 credits)
- CS 310: Database Systems (4 credits)
- CS 320: Software Engineering (4 credits)
- CS 330: Computer Networks (4 credits)
- CS 340: Theory of Computation (4 credits)
- CS 401: Compiler Design (4 credits)
- CS 410: Artificial Intelligence (4 credits)
- CS 490: Senior Capstone Project (8 credits)

4. GPA Requirements
4.1 Cumulative GPA: Minimum 2.0 (on a 4.0 scale)
4.2 Major GPA: Minimum 2.5 in all CS-prefixed courses
4.3 Students falling below 2.0 cumulative GPA for two consecutive semesters will be placed on academic probation.

5. Capstone Project
5.1 All students must complete a senior capstone project (CS 490) in their final year.
5.2 The project must demonstrate integration of at least three core areas of computer science.
5.3 Projects are evaluated by a panel of three faculty members.
5.4 A written thesis (minimum 40 pages) and oral defense are required.

6. Residency Requirement
Students must complete at least 32 of their final 48 credit hours at Acme University. Transfer credits may be applied to earlier coursework with departmental approval.

7. Application for Graduation
Students must submit their Intent to Graduate form through the Registrar's Office by:
- October 15 for Spring graduation
- March 15 for Fall graduation
- June 1 for Summer graduation

8. Honors Designation
- Cum Laude: GPA 3.5 - 3.69
- Magna Cum Laude: GPA 3.7 - 3.89
- Summa Cum Laude: GPA 3.9 - 4.0
